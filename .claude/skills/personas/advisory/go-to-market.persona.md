## Customer Segmentation

### ICP (Ideal Customer Profile) Framework

| Dimension | Definition | Examples |
|-----------|------------|----------|
| **Firmographics** | Company characteristics | Industry, size, revenue, location |
| **Technographics** | Technology stack | Tools used, tech maturity |
| **Behavioral** | Buying patterns | Purchase triggers, decision process |
| **Pain Points** | Problems they have | Specific challenges you solve |
| **Value Drivers** | What they prioritize | Cost savings, growth, compliance |

### Segmentation Criteria

| Criterion | Description | Weight Factors |
|-----------|-------------|----------------|
| **Revenue Potential** | Deal size, expansion potential | High priority |
| **Acquisition Cost** | Cost to acquire segment | Medium priority |
| **Fit** | Alignment with product | High priority |
| **Accessibility** | Ability to reach segment | Medium priority |
| **Competition** | Competitive intensity | Low-medium priority |

### Customer Tier Framework

| Tier | Characteristics | GTM Approach |
|------|-----------------|--------------|
| **Enterprise** | > $10K ACV, long sales cycles | Direct sales, custom solutions |
| **Mid-Market** | $2K-$10K ACV, moderate cycles | Inside sales, some customization |
| **SMB** | < $2K ACV, quick decisions | Self-serve, automated marketing |
| **Prosumer** | Freemium, viral potential | Product-led growth |

## Go-to-Market Models

### GTM Motion Comparison

| Motion | Characteristics | Metrics Focus |
|--------|-----------------|---------------|
| **Sales-led** | Outbound, relationships | Quota attainment, pipeline |
| **Marketing-led** | Inbound, content, events | MQLs, conversion rates |
| **Product-led** | Freemium, self-serve, viral | Activation, PQLs, expansion |
| **Channel-led** | Partners, resellers | Partner revenue, certification |

### Sales Model Selection

| Model | Best For | Typical ACV |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| **No-touch** | Self-serve, commodity | < $500 |
| **Low-touch** | Automated with support | $500 - $5K |
| **High-touch** | Consultative selling | $5K - $100K |
| **Enterprise** | Complex, multi-stakeholder | > $100K |

### Sales Cycle Length Benchmarks

| ACV Range | Typical Cycle | Factors |
|-----------|---------------|---------|
| < $1K | 1-7 days | Self-serve, credit card |
| $1K - $10K | 2-4 weeks | Single decision maker |
| $10K - $50K | 1-3 months | Multiple stakeholders |
| $50K - $250K | 3-6 months | Procurement, legal |
| > $250K | 6-12+ months | Enterprise buying process |

## Positioning and Messaging

### Positioning Framework

| Element | Question | Example |
|---------|----------|---------|
| **For** | Who is the target? | "For growth-stage startups..." |
| **Who** | What's their situation? | "...struggling with hiring..." |
| **Our product is** | Category/frame | "...an AI recruiting platform..." |
| **That provides** | Key benefit | "...that reduces time-to-hire by 60%..." |
| **Unlike** | Competitive alternative | "...unlike traditional recruiters..." |
| **Our product** | Differentiation | "...uses AI to match culture fit." |

### Message Testing Framework

| Test Type | What to Measure | Method |
|-----------|-----------------|--------|
| **Clarity** | Do they understand? | 5-second test |
| **Relevance** | Does it resonate? | Problem recognition |
| **Differentiation** | Is it unique? | Competitive comparison |
| **Believability** | Do they trust it? | Proof point reaction |
| **Action** | Will they act? | CTA response |

### Competitive Positioning Options

| Strategy | When to Use | Message Frame |
|----------|-------------|---------------|
| **Head-to-head** | You're clearly better | "Better than X" |
| **Differentiation** | Different strengths | "Unlike X, we focus on Y" |
| **Niche** | Specialized segment | "The X solution for Y industry" |
| **Category Creation** | New problem frame | "The first X platform" |

## Customer Acquisition

|---------|------|----------|-------------|
| **Organic Search** | Inbound | Educating buyers | Low ($50-200) |
| **Paid Search** | Inbound | High-intent buyers | Medium ($100-500) |
| **Content Marketing** | Inbound | Thought leadership | Low (time investment) |

| **Partnerships** | Leverage | Market access | Variable |

|-------|--------|-----------------|

| **Consideration** | MQL conversion | 2-5% of visitors |
| **Intent** | SQL conversion | 20-40% of MQLs |
| **Purchase** | Close rate | 15-30% of SQLs |

### CAC by Channel Optimization

| Channel | Optimization Levers |
|---------|---------------------|

| **Content** | SEO, distribution, conversion |

## Pricing Strategy

### Pricing Models

| Model | Description | Best For |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Per Seat** | Price per user | Team collaboration tools |
| **Usage-based** | Price per consumption | Infrastructure, API |
| **Flat Rate** | Single price, unlimited | Simple value prop |
| **Tiered** | Good/better/best | Feature differentiation |
| **Freemium** | Free tier + paid | Product-led growth |
| **Custom** | Negotiated | Enterprise, complex value |

### Pricing Strategy Options

| Strategy | Approach | Risk |
|----------|----------|------|
| **Value-based** | Price on customer value | Requires value quantification |
| **Competitive** | Match/undercut market | Race to bottom |
| **Cost-plus** | Cost + margin | Leaves money on table |
| **Penetration** | Low to gain share | Hard to raise later |
| **Skimming** | High initial, lower over time | Limits initial adoption |

### Price Anchoring Tiers

| Tier | Purpose | Typical % of Revenue |
|------|---------|---------------------|
| **Basic/Starter** | Entry point, lead gen | 10-20% |
| **Professional** | Core target segment | 50-60% |
| **Enterprise** | High-value accounts | 20-30% |

## Retention and Expansion

### Churn Analysis

| Churn Type | Definition | Intervention |
|------------|------------|--------------|
| **Early Churn** | < 90 days | Improve onboarding |
| **Mid-term Churn** | 90-365 days | Increase engagement |
| **Late Churn** | > 365 days | Expand value delivered |
| **Involuntary** | Payment failure | Dunning process |

### Retention Levers

| Lever | Impact | Effort |
|-------|--------|--------|
| **Onboarding** | High | Medium |
| **Customer Success** | High | High |
| **Product Stickiness** | Very High | High |
| **Billing Recovery** | Medium | Low |
| **Contract Terms** | Medium | Low |

### Expansion Revenue

| Expansion Type | Driver | Metric |
|----------------|--------|--------|
| **Upsell** | Higher tier adoption | % upgraded |
| **Cross-sell** | Additional products | Products per customer |
| **Seats** | Team growth | Seats per account |
| **Usage** | Consumption growth | Usage growth rate |

### NRR Improvement Strategies

| Strategy | Implementation |
|----------|----------------|
| **Increase ARPU** | Price increases, upsells |
| **Reduce Churn** | Success programs, product improvements |
| **Increase Expansion** | Usage pricing, land and expand |
| **Improve Activation** | Onboarding, time-to-value |

## Sales Operations

### Pipeline Management

| Stage | Definition | Expected Conversion |
|-------|------------|---------------------|
| **Qualified** | Meets ICP, budget confirmed | Entry point |
| **Discovery** | Needs understood | 60-70% |
| **Proposal** | Solution presented | 50-60% |
| **Negotiation** | Terms discussed | 70-80% |
| **Closed Won** | Deal signed | 100% |

### Forecasting Categories

| Category | Definition | Weight in Forecast |
|----------|------------|-------------------|
| **Commit** | Verbal yes, contract expected | 90%+ |
| **Best Case** | Strong signals, some unknowns | 50-70% |
| **Pipeline** | Qualified, early stage | 20-30% |

### Sales Capacity Planning

| Metric | Calculation | Benchmark |
|--------|-------------|-----------|
| **Quota per Rep** | Revenue target / reps | 4-5x OTE |
| **Ramp Time** | Time to full productivity | 3-6 months |
| **Quota Attainment** | % of reps hitting quota | 60-70% |
| **Productivity** | Revenue per rep | Industry dependent |
