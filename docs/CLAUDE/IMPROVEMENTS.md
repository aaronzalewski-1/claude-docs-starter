## How to Use This Document

This document tracks potential improvements, refactoring ideas, and architectural changes. Use it to:
- Capture ideas without immediately implementing them
- Evaluate trade-offs before committing to changes
- Track what was considered and why decisions were made
- Provide context for future developers (or AI assistants)

---

## Proposal Template

Use this format when adding new proposals:

```markdown
### [IMP-XXX] Title

**Status:** Proposed | In Progress | Completed | Rejected
**Priority:** P0 (Critical) | P1 (High) | P2 (Medium) | P3 (Low)
**Effort:** Small (hours) | Medium (days) | Large (weeks)

**Problem Statement:**
What issue does this address? Why is it worth solving?

**Proposed Solution:**
How should we solve it? What's the approach?

**Alternatives Considered:**
What other approaches were evaluated? Why were they rejected?

**Implementation Notes:**
Any technical details, constraints, or dependencies.

**Decision:**
[If completed/rejected] What was decided and why?
```

## Quick Reference: Priority Levels

| Priority | Description | Timeline |
|----------|-------------|----------|
| P0 | Critical - Blocking development or causing major issues | Immediate |
| P1 | High - Important for upcoming work or significant improvement | This sprint |
| P2 | Medium - Would be nice, improves quality of life | Next sprint |
| P3 | Low - Future consideration, nice to have | Backlog |
